About

Why WordNerd?

Most flashcard apps are just binary — right or wrong. But language doesn't work like that. WordNerd scores your translations on a spectrum, so "friendship" for "companionship" isn't a failure, it's close.

3-day cycles

Each word goes through daily quizzes over 3 days. Once you've scored high enough across all three, it's gone from your queue.

Non-binary feedback

Your translation gets embedded with a multilingual transformer and compared to the target word. The score reflects how close you actually were.

Leveled word lists

Words are split into Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced. You pick how many batches per day — fits a 5-minute session or a longer one.

Skip what you know

Before each cycle starts, you can tap out the words you already know. No time wasted on things you've already got down.
